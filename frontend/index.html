About WaferMap AI

This system utilizes an EfficientNet-B0 model trained on the WM-811K dataset to automatically classify semiconductor wafer maps into 9 distinct defect categories.

It also features Grad-CAM spatial localization to highlight exactly where the AI detects anomalies, aiding root-cause analysis.
